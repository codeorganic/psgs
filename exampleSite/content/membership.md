New members are welcome (see membership form). Benefits of membership include a library of
gesneriad reference materials, access to rare and unusual gesneriads, and a source for hard to obtain
growing supplies. We hold an annual show where members display plants and provide information to
the public. There is also a plant sale open to the public.

Membership in the international Gesneriad Society includes a journal, Gesneriads, published four
times a year. [Their website](https://www.gesneriadsociety.org/) includes extensive information about Gesneriads. They
also sponsor an annual convention hosted by a different chapter each year. PSGS will host the
convention in 2022.
